Business Master

Manage your business and try to survive!

How to play:
  • Click on the bowl of noodles to make a $15 bowl of noodles. Click on the cup of coffee to make a $10 cup of black coffee.
  • You can adjust your profit by pressing 1 to increase by one dollar and 0 to decrease by one dollar.
  • Each customer is sure to have a bowl of noodles and may have a cup of coffee. Press the spacebar to sell to the customer.
  • Mark only sells noodles, it takes Mark a while to increase his profit and doesn't increase it more than $4 so you always have an advantage.
  • A business day lasts for 2 minutes, the player is sent home and needs to pay three bills: rent $40, food $10 and electricity $20.
